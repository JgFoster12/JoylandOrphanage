Joyland Orphanage Kenya is dedicated to providing each orphan with every need while training him or her in the ways of the LORD.

A SHELTER IN THE STORM - ISAIAH 4:6


JOYLAND ORPHANAGE is a non-profit ministry which Pastor Dalmas Omwega and Esther Michael started in 2016. The Lord gave Pastor Dalmas a vision to help children with no homes and gave him the ministry name "Joyland Orphanage." Since the political crisis of 2017, Pastor Dalmas and his church family have dedicated their lives to caring for children who lost their parents. Joyland Orphanage cares for 35 children ranging in ages 4 to 12 years old. Pastor Dalmas and his church take care as many orphans as the Lord sends his way. When they come through the doors of Joyland Orphanage, Pastor Dalmas and team do everything in their power to provide for, educate, and disciple the children.
